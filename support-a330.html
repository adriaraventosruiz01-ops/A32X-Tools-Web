About the App

What aircraft does A330 Tools cover?

A330 Tools covers the Airbus A330 family, including A330-200 and A330-300 variants with both CF6 and Trent 700 engine types. All content is based on standard A330 series documentation used in commercial airline line maintenance operations.

Which ATA chapters and systems are included?

Core coverage includes ECAM messages and reset procedures for the main line maintenance ATAs on the A330: Air Conditioning (21), Auto Flight (22), Electrical Power (24), Fire Protection (26), Flight Controls (27), Fuel (28), Hydraulic (29), Ice & Rain Protection (30), Instruments (31), Landing Gear (32), Lights (33), Navigation (34), and Pneumatic (36), among others. The electrical contacts section covers ESPM connector, splice, and terminal references specific to the A330.

How complete is the ECAM content for the A330?

The app covers the most frequently encountered A330 ECAM messages and reset procedures in day-to-day line maintenance. It is a continuously updated field reference tool — always cross-check critical procedures against the latest official Airbus documentation before performing any maintenance task.

How is A330 Tools different from A32X Tools?

They are separate apps for different aircraft types. A330 Tools is built for the Airbus A330, while A32X Tools covers the A320 family (A318, A319, A320, A321). The feature set is identical — ECAM resets, damage reports, electrical contacts, AMM references, alpha callups, and part numbers — but all technical content is aircraft-specific to each type.

How It Works

Does the app work offline?

Yes, A330 Tools is fully offline. All A330 ECAM messages, reset procedures, electrical contacts, alpha callups, and part numbers are stored locally on your device from the moment you install the app. No internet connection is required for any feature.

How do damage reports and PDF export work?

You can log multiple damage findings in a single inspection event, attach photos from your camera or gallery, and specify zone and damage type. The app generates a structured PDF report that can be shared directly via email, AirDrop, or any compatible app — no account or internet connection needed.

What are custom items and how do I use them?

Custom items let you add your own entries — a specific A330 part number, a fleet-specific procedure, or a local reference — with photos and notes. Your entries are saved locally and can be exported and shared with colleagues. Especially useful for extending the built-in A330 catalog with airline-specific references.

Technical & Data Privacy

Where is my data stored?

All user-generated data — custom items, saved damage reports, and app preferences — is stored locally on your device using the platform's standard secure storage. A330 Tools does not use cloud servers, and your data never leaves your device.

How do I report a bug or issue?

Email us at info@a32xtools.com with your device model, operating system version, the app version (visible in the app settings), and a brief description of steps to reproduce the issue. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Is my data secure?

Yes. A330 Tools does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or user-generated content. All information stays on your device, protected by your operating system's security model. No account or registration is required to use the app.
