Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-27

A32X Tools respects your privacy. This app is designed to work without collecting personal information from users.

1) Personal data

A32X Tools does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or advertising identifiers.

The app does not require user accounts or registration.

2) Camera and photos

The camera is used only when the user chooses to add a photo to a custom part number or damage report.

Photos are stored locally on the device. Photos are not uploaded, shared, or transmitted by the app.

3) Internet access

Internet access is used only to download updates for the internal part number database.

The app does not send personal data as part of these updates.
Note: like most internet services, standard server logs may temporarily process technical information (such as IP address) for security and delivery purposes.

4) Local storage

All user-created data (custom part numbers, damage reports and related photos) is stored locally on the device.

A32X Tools does not use cloud storage for user content.

5) Third-party services

A32X Tools does not use advertising SDKs.

A32X Tools does not use analytics or tracking services.

6) Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app functionality changes. The latest version will be made available at this page.

7) Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer at:
info@a320xtools.com
